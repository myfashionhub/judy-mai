Welcome to Mai World

Judy Mai was born in Hai Phong, Vietnam and raised in Germany. Mai’s studies began 11 years ago.

At age 12, she commenced singing lessons in Frankfurt, Germany where she studied both classical and pop music. When Judy Mai was in 7th grade, she was a soloist at a Gospel Choir at school, which laid the foundation of her passion for Soul and R’n’B music.

She gained a lot of live experience through performing as a solo singer and touring with her dance group and Gospel Choir nationally.
