Biography

Judy Mai is a Vietnamese French singer/songwriter based in Berlin, Germany. Her musical style is a mix of R'n'B, Soul and Pop. She draws inspiration from female acts with powerful voices, including Whitney Houston, Mariah Carey, Celine Dion, Christina Aguilera and Beyonce. She has written her own material since she was 15 and performed live in many countries over the years, including Austria, Vietname, US, Hungary, etc.

At 16, after graduating high school, Mai moved to Orange County, California, where she began performing at weddings, night clubs and various venues and functions. Her voice and melody can be found in the hooks of several American and German rappers' tracks. Since 2010, she has participated and earned high marks in televised talent shows and singing contests in Vietnam, Germany and the Czech Republic.

Mai has wanted to pursue a singing career for as long as she could remember. She began vocal training at the age of 12 in Frankfurt, Germany and was a soloist in her school's gospel choir. Mai has attended Noisy Academy in Berlin and spent one year honing her craft at the Military University of Culture and Art in Hanoi, Vietnam.

Mai's first single Look At Me, produced by DJ Seip and written by herself, will be released in October 2015. Following the buzz of this single, Mai hopes to start recording an album and perform as much as she can.

Not only is she a talented singer, Mai also speaks 5 languages, including English, German, Vietnamese, Chinese and French. Her second passion to music is dancing, and she taught streetstyle hip hop for five years. Mai has her family, friends and producers to thank for helping her to where she is today. Among them is a magic maker, hair stylist and makeup artist Michael Adrian.
