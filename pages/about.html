Biography

Judy Mai is a Berlin-based Vietnamese German singer/songwriter whose musical style is a mix of R'n'B, Soul and Pop. She draws inspiration from female acts with powerful voices, including Whitney Houston, Mariah Carey, Celine Dion, Christina Aguilera and Beyonce.

Mai has wanted to pursue a singing career for as long as she could remember. She began vocal training at the age of 12 in Frankfurt, Germany and was a soloist in her school's gospel choir. At 16, she moved to Orange County, California after graduating high school and started performing at weddings, nightclubs, and other functions.

Judy Mai began writing her own material at 22 and has collaborated with several American and German rappers (writing and vocal) on the hook of their tracks. Since 2010, she has participated and earned high marks in televised talent shows and singing contests in Vietnam, Germany and the Czech Republic.

Mai's first single Look At Me, produced by DJ Seip and written by herself, will be released in September 2015. Following the buzz of this single, Mai hopes to start recording an album and perform as much as she can.

Not only is she a talented singer, Mai also speaks 5 languages, including English, German, Vietnamese, Chinese and French. Her second passion to music is dancing, and she taught streetstyle hip hop for five years. Mai loves traveling and enjoying good food, especially that of the fusion/creative variety. On the weekend, she's likely to be found at Berlin's hip hop clubs Avenue, Tube Station or Prince Charles, after dining on Asian fusion cuisine with her group of friends.

Mai has her family, friends and producers to thank for helping her to where she is today. Among them is a magic maker, hair stylist and makeup artist Michael Adrian.
