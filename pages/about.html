About Judy Mai

Judy Mai was born in Hai Phong, Vietnam and raised in Germany. Mai’s studies began 11 years ago. At age 12, she commenced singing lessons in Frankfurt, Germany where she studied both classical and pop music. When Judy Mai was in 7th grade she was a soloist at a Gospel Choir at school, which laid the foundation of her passion for Soul and R’n’B music. She gained a lot of stage experience through performing as a solo singer and touring with her dance group and Gospel Choir nationally.

After graduating High School at the age of 16, Mai relocated to Orange County, California. As her uncle was a keyboarder in a Band, she had the opportunity to do shows every weekend as well as to perform in weddings, clubs and other functions throughout the O.C. and Los Angeles areas.

When she returned to Germany, Judy Mai began recording many songs which she had written over the past years. In 2008 she participated at a singing competition on television and won first prize. After that Mai decided to go to Vietnam to study music. She completed a one-year vocal program in classical and contemporary music.
